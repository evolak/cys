Vision

That every young person will develop an enviable character based on sound moral values that will propel them to impact their environment positively

That every young person will be armed with information on as many health and behavioural issues as possible

To equip young people in Nigeria with enough information to make wholesome decisions that would positively impact their lifes, communities and the country.

Mission

  • empower young people in Nigeria through life skills trainings
  • To arm young people with information on health and behavioural issues
  • To provide a platform for Research and information-driven policies for the welfare of youths in Nigeria
