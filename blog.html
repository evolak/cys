ABOUT THE HUB

THE HUB is designed as an after school and weekend training programme for secondary school students and youths in different localities in the Country.
It can however be effectively run during the long (summer) school vacations.

CORE SUBJECTS

  • Leadership Training
  • Life Skills Training (Character Develoment)
  • Creative and Performing Arts Training

VISION

To raise young people who have developed admirable character to impact the world of the performing arts

Mission

To raise up a new breed of practitioners in the performing arts industry; people who through exemplary character use their craft to build a better society.

About Us
