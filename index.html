Vision To equip young people in Nigeria through life skills training to impact their communities and the Country positively.

Mission

  • To empower young people in Nigeria through life skills training.
  • To arm young people with information on health and behavioural issues.
  • To provide a platform for Research and Information-driven Policies for the welfare of Youths in Nigeria.

What We Do

Ensure education for sustainable lifestyles through character development (life skills) training, equipping young people with information on health and behavioral issues and drama training.

Life's Skill Training

Life skills training empowers people to develop coping and self-management skills needed for healthy and productive lives.

Performing Arts Training

Trainings and Workshops on different areas of creative expression Dance, Music, Artwork, Make-Up, Etc.

Drama

DRAMA: Production of issues-related drama for stage, radio,television and other media

Dissemination of information on health and behavioural issues

Drama competition among secondary schools

Research

To provide a platform for Research and information-driven policies for the welfare of youths in Nigeria

About Us

CENTRE FOR YOUTH STUDIES [a.k.a. THE CENTRE] is a non Governmental Organisation registered in Nigeria, West Africa. (CAC/IT/NO. 23191)
